Html Forms

Forms are used by html pages to receive information produced by the user and to relay the information back to the server. With the information being prependded by the form tag. At the the server the information is handled by the backend infrastructure and a new webpage is sent back to confirm that the information was received.

Example Form

Forms are flagged at the beginning and end by the "form" tag, at which point the form section uses the "input" tag with input parameters following the word word within the tag. Standard with all inputs is type and name. Type desrcibes the interface being made (button, box, etc) and name references where the input will headed to on the server.
