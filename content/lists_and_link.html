Links

Links are a core feature of the web allowing individuals to move between a myriad different webpages, giving the rise to the idea ofweb surfing or browsing. Links to other pages make use of the a tag with [href="..."] following in toe within the tag as a way to define where the link will lead with test in between the tags rendering what will be displayed on the html doc.

a href="/index.html"/a. Index is a standard link leading to another webpage.

Mailto: links

href mailto bnejal@gmail.com creates a link that spawns the local computer's email client and uses the client to create an email pointed at the email address
